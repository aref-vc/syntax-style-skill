Before · default
The founder walked in. She was late. She was underslept. She was carrying three coffees.
→
After · crafted
The founder walked in late and underslept, three coffees balanced in two hands, her laptop bag slipping off one shoulder, already apologizing to no one in particular.
The move: four clipped sentences become one base clause with free modifiers trailing off it. The fix for choppy prose is a longer, well-built sentence, not a shorter one.
