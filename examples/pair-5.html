Short · Marketing · Syntactic symbolism (increase)
Before · default
The approval process kept getting more complicated and slower over time until it barely worked.
→
After · crafted
Step by step, approval by approval, sign-off stacked on sign-off, the process grew until it could no longer move.
The move: the accreting parallel phrases enact the pile-up they describe. The sentence gets heavier as the process does. Symbolism here is increase, not acceleration.
